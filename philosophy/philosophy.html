“Everything is theoretically impossible, until it is done”

Innovate when Necessary

We avoid reinventing the wheel and ensure the prior art is exhausted. The most exciting innovations are built on a solid foundation.

Comprehensibility first

We prioritise determinism and certainty over indeterminism and uncertainty. Statistical approaches are helpful in situations with hidden variables and genuine uncertainty.

Quality

“Proof of concept” does not have to mean low quality. We work assuming commercialisation is just over the horizon. Moreover, the rushing of sensitive physics or statistical code is a false economy, normally resulting in corrective steps that are even more costly.

Contrarian

Sometimes a contrarian approach is needed to get an advantage over the competition. We are not afraid to challenge the dogma of the day.

Empirical

We focus on what’s true in practice rather than esoteric theory.
