Simulation Engineer:

Location: Fully remote

Salary range: Competitive salary

Type: Full-time/Contract

Job description:

  • Develop, validate and use simulation models to predict the behaviour of systems.
  • Use both data-driven and physics based modelling techniques where appropriate.
  • Collaborate to plan validation activities for models across a range of test environments
  • Use data science and machine learning techniques to improve the accuracy and efficiency of simulation models.
  • Participate in code reviews and provide constructive feedback to team members

Requirements:

  • Strong understanding of engineering principles.
  • Solid understanding of the science of Modelling and Simulation, and demonstratable experience of leading simulation activities in a commercial setting.
  • Experience with data science and machine learning techniques.
  • Strong comprehension of statistics, data analytics and design of experiments.
  • Genuine interest and experience in solving industry problems using scientific software
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills, enabling effective collaboration and information sharing.
  • Demonstrated enthusiasm for ongoing learning and a proactive attitude towards adopting new technologies.
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, adaptable, and high-pressure environment, delivering results efficiently.
